💡 Trivia & Did You Know
▼
Casting: Michael Keaton was originally considered for the lead role, before having to drop out over schedule conflicts and Mark Ruffalo being ultimately cast instead.
Production: The series was filmed entirely on location in the Philadelphia suburbs, including Coatesville, Pennsylvania, to maintain authenticity.
Connections: Created by Brad Ingelsby, who also created the critically acclaimed "Mare of Easttown" starring Kate Winslet.
Goofs: Pennsylvania locals pronounce Lancaster as "LANK-iss-ter", with the emphasis on the first syllable; however the characters pronounce it as Lan-CAST-er, with the emphasis on the middle syllable.
Awards: Mark Ruffalo received Emmy nomination for Outstanding Lead Actor in a Limited Series for his portrayal of Tom Brandis.
